Instructions

This recipe goes beyond the basics, inviting you to indulge in the richness of a velvety chocolate mousse that melts in your mouth with every spoonful. It's a celebration of simplicity, where every ingredient plays a crucial role in creating a dessert that is as comforting as it is delightful.

Allow the mousse to chill for at least two hours before serving. This resting period is essential; it allows the flavors to meld together, ensuring each bite is creamy and bursting with chocolate decadence. As you dip your spoon into the smooth surface, be prepared for the enticing aroma and taste that signals your Chocolate Mousse is ready to be savored.

Preparation

  1. Place the sugar in the bowl and pulverize for 10 seconds at speed 10. Use a spatula to scrape down the sides of the bowl.
  2. Add the chocolate and cream, and melt for 4 minutes at 50°C on speed 2.
  3. Add the egg yolks and mix for 15 seconds at speed 4. Remove and set aside.
  4. In a clean, dry bowl, insert the whisk attachment. Add the egg whites and salt, and beat for 3 minutes at speed 3.5. Gently fold the beaten egg whites into the chocolate mixture using a spatula. Divide into 6 cups and refrigerate for about 3 hours before serving.

Ingredients

  • 50 g sugar
  • 200 g cooking chocolate (min. 50% cocoa), chopped
  • 100 g cream (30% fat)
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 4 egg whites
  • 1 pinch of salt

Details

  • Category: Dessert
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Preparation Time: 10 min
  • Total Time: 3 h 20 min
  • Servings: 6 portions
